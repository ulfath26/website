UTTHARAPRADESH

marble mosque

The most venerated mosque shrine in Uttarakhand is Piran Kaliyar Sharif located towards south of Haridwar which is equally revered by Hindus and Muslims. It witnesses large number of devotees every year seeking blessings from The Almighty. Piran Kaliyar is the dargah of Alauddin Ali Ahmed Sabir Kalyari.
